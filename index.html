Mission & Vision

Mitigating aquatic pollution through autonomous, sustainable technology.

The Mission

To restore the purity of our waterways by deploying intelligent, solar-powered robotics that autonomously combat marine pollution, protecting aquatic life and securing a healthier future for coastal communities.

The Vision

To become the leading provider of autonomous marine environmental services globally, setting the standard for automated pollution control.

The CleanSea Interceptor

A "Roomba-style" automated solution for urban waterways.

AI-Powered Recognition

Equipped with edge-AI processing to detect and classify floating waste (plastics, styrofoam, nylon) in real-time.

Solar-Driven Autonomy

Operates independently of the grid using high-efficiency solar panels and a LiFePO₄ battery storage system.

Automated Conveyor

Features a front-loading conveyor belt that actively scoops floating debris into a detachable onboard holding bin.

Our Business Models

Hybrid B2B/B2G solutions for sustainable impact.

Cleanup-as-a-Service

Clients pay a fixed "Clean Water Fee" for a debris-free zone. Ideal for Hotels and Private Resorts.

Direct Sales & Leasing

Outright purchase or long-term leasing options for government agencies to deploy fleets across public waterways.

Data Monetization

We sell "Pollution Insight Reports" regarding waste density and flow patterns to research institutions.
