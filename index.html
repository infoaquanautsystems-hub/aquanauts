How It Works

The Technology Behind the Bot

Each AquaNaut unit combines robotics, AI navigation, and IoT data reporting into a single waterproof system.

🤖

Autonomous Navigation

GPS-guided path planning with obstacle avoidance allows bots to cover their 450m² zone without any manual control.

🌊

Waste Collection System

Onboard conveyor and filtration system scoops floating debris, plastics, and organic waste into sealed holding tanks.

📡

Real-Time IoT Data

Each bot reports its GPS location, waste collected, battery level, and water quality readings every 30 seconds via cellular.

🔋

Solar + Battery Hybrid

Solar panels keep each unit running for up to 18 hours per day. Auto-dock recharging ensures continuous operation.

📊

Live Dashboard

Customers and admins access a real-time command centre showing bot locations, waste totals, and fleet health at a glance.

🛡️

Weatherproof Design

IP68-rated chassis built for tropical conditions — heavy rain, humidity, and tidal variation. Designed and tested in Lagos.
